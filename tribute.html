Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was known for his role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.

Early Life

Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Kalam came from a humble background. He studied physics and aerospace engineering and later joined DRDO and ISRO.
